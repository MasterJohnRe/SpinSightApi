🔒 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 18.06.2025

This Chrome Extension (“the Extension”) respects your privacy and is designed to operate fully locally on your browser without collecting or storing personal information.

✅ What We Collect:

  • We do not collect, transmit, use or store:
    • Your identity or browser history
    • Login credentials or personal information
    • Any interactions outside the game page

❌ No User Data Storage

  • We do not store any data on our servers.

💬 Optional Tipping

Users may choose to support the extension by voluntarily clicking a Tip button. This redirects to a publicly visible tipping page hosted outside the extension. No user tracking is associated with this feature.

🧾 Changes

We may update this policy in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
